Community Bonding — Week 1 (18 May – 24 May)

Community bonding officially began. I introduced myself to the Liquid Galaxy community, joined Slack/Discord channels, and set up meetings with my mentor Marc Gonzalez Capdevila.

This week I:

  • Read Liquid Galaxy architecture docs — master node, display nodes, Google Earth sync.
  • Studied how KML / KMZ tours are traditionally copied and launched by hand.
  • Cloned related Liquid Galaxy tooling repos and ran a local Node.js playground.
  • Drafted the problem statement: remove manual SSH + file transfer from tour ops.
  • Agreed with Marc on the tech stack — Express, MongoDB, Socket.IO, ssh2, Multer, JWT.

Next week: finalize the module breakdown (auth, upload, SSH, realtime) and repo skeleton.
